My Recipes

An entry in My Recipes can be any combination of different foods you choose. It can be simple, like a turkey sandwich (consisting of one slice of turkey plus two slices of rye bread), or more complex, such as a casserole dish with many ingredients. If you choose, a recipe can also represent a meal (for example, "breakfast" consisting of two eggs, toast, coffee, and juice).
